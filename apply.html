Apply to a Head Start Grant

Head Start programs are built through grassroot efforts to apply for federal funding. Organizations applying must have a track record in the community proposed to be served and the community must have eligible families to enroll in Head Start. Learn more about eligible institutions to build Head Start programs in your neighborhood.

Government

State, Tribal, County, City, and Township governments are eligible.

Housing Authorities

Public Housing Authorities and Indian Housing Authorities are eligible.

Education Institutions

Independent School Districts as well as Public, Private, or State- funded Higher Education Institutions are eligible.

Private Sector

Nonprofits with 501(c)(3) status in addition to For-profit organizations and small businesses are eligible.

New Mexico Head Start Grant

The Office of Head Start has allocated funding for New Mexico Head Start and Early Head Start grantees. Eligible Counties include: Ariba, Bernalillo, Curry, De Baca, Grant, Guadalupe, Harding, Quay, Roosevelt, Rio, and Taos.
