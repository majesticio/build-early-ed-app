Head Start Enrollment Eligibility

The Department of Health and Human Services Poverty Guidelines are used to determine eligibility for Head Start programs. If you are currently pregnant or have children from birth to age 5, check the requirements below to see if your family is eligible.

Child's Age

Head Start serves children ages 0-5. Prenatal services are also provided.

Household Income

Head Start serves families with household incomes at or below the federal poverty line.

Federal Benefits

Beneficiaries of Temporary Assistance for Needy Families or Social Security Income are eligible.

Household Status

If your family is experiencing homelessness or providing foster care, then your child is eligible to enroll.
